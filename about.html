About Breakfast to Breakfast Restaurant

​Located in the heart of Accra’s vibrant Osu district, Breakfast to Breakfast (BtoB) is a 24/7 culinary haven that blends international flavors with Ghanaian warmth. Whether you're craving a hearty English breakfast, a Lebanese manakish, or a juicy burger, BtoB’s extensive menu caters to every palate at any hour. The relaxed, modern ambiance—complete with indoor and outdoor seating—makes it the perfect spot for early risers, night owls, and everyone in between. Known for its friendly service and diverse offerings, BtoB is more than just a restaurant—it’s a go-to destination for comfort food, community, and convenience in Accra.

Our mission is to offer a unique dining experience with top-notch service, a cozy atmosphere, and the freshest ingredients. Join us today and enjoy an unforgettable experience.
